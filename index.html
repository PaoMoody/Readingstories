Max and the Magic Box

MAX had a BIG BOX.
He liked HIS BOX a lot.
IT is my fun BOX,” he said.
One day, MAX put the BOX in a DEN.
He said, "This is the best SPOT.”
What was in the BOX?
A WIG, a KITE, and a BONE!
MAX got a MAP.
The MAP had a red DOT.
"What is at the red DOT?" MAX said.
"I must go see."
He got his CAP.
He got a CUP.
Then he got on his BIKE.
He had to HOP, JOG, and RUN.
"That DOT is far!" said MAX.
On the way, MAX met a PIG.
"Do you like fun?" MAX said.
"Yes!" said the PIG.
"Come with me," said MAX.
Then they met a BUG.
The BUG had a BIG NAP.
"Do you like fun too?" said MAX.
The BUG said, "Yes! Let's go!"
They got to the red DOT.
The BOX was on a ROCK!
"Oh wow!" said the PIG.
"What is in it?" said the BUG.
MAX said, "A KITE, a WIG, and a BONE!"
The BUG put on the WIG.
The PIG got the KITE.
MAX got the BONE.
They RAN, SAT, and had fun in the SUN.
"It is the best day," said MAX.
SUN went down.
"It is time to go," said MAX.
He put the BOX in a CAVE.
"Let's go back soon," he said.
They all went HOME.
It was a very good day.
The end.
